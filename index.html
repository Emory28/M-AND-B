Our Services

Everything you need to keep your home, office, and pet in great shape.

🏠 Residential Cleaning

We keep your home spotless on a schedule that works for you — weekly, fortnightly, or monthly.

  • Kitchen & bathroom deep clean
  • Vacuuming & mopping all floors
  • Dusting surfaces & skirting boards
  • Eco-friendly products available

🏢 Office Cleaning

A clean workspace improves productivity. We handle your office before or after business hours.

  • Desks, phones & keyboards sanitised
  • Kitchen & breakroom cleaned
  • Bins emptied & replaced
  • Flexible early morning or evening slots

✨ Deep Cleaning

A thorough, intensive clean for homes needing a fresh start — ideal after moving in or out.

  • Inside ovens, fridges & cupboards
  • Limescale removal in bathrooms
  • Behind & under furniture cleaned
  • Window sill & frame scrubbing

🐾 Dog Walking

Your dog deserves fresh air and exercise every day. Our dog walkers are caring, reliable, and DBS checked.

  • 30 or 60-minute walks available
  • Solo or group walks
  • Photo updates sent after each walk
  • Fully insured dog walkers

  • Grocery shopping & collection
  • Laundry & ironing assistance
  • Post office, pharmacy runs
  • Light decluttering & organising
